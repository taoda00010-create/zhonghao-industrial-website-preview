Privacy Notice

How we handle RFQ information

This notice applies to information submitted through the quotation forms on this website.

Information we collect

We collect the product, quantity, destination, specification, packaging, Incoterm, company, contact name, business email, WhatsApp number, message, source page and privacy confirmation that you choose to submit.

Why we use it

We use the information only to review your industrial-material requirement, clarify technical or commercial details, prepare a quotation discussion, prevent abuse and maintain the inquiry record.

Form processing

Our static forms are processed by Basin, which acts as a form-processing service provider and provides submission storage, email notification and spam protection. Your information is not automatically added to a marketing list.

Retention

An inquiry that does not become an order is retained for up to 12 months and then deleted during the monthly record review. If a transaction is completed, relevant customer and transaction records may be retained separately as required for the business relationship and applicable record-keeping duties.

Security and spam protection

The form may use a hidden honeypot, Cloudflare Turnstile and Basin validation or filtering. These controls help distinguish genuine inquiries from automated abuse.

Your choices

Privacy consent allows us to respond to the RFQ; it is not marketing consent. To request access, correction or deletion of an unconverted inquiry, email eric@smzhenergy.com.
