Semi Coke for Calcium Carbide Production: RFQ and Burden Checklist

How to discuss Semi Coke with reference to carbide furnace practice, lime burden, available carbon, impurities, moisture and particle size.

Shenmu Zhonghao Editorial TeamReviewed by: EricUpdated: 7/16/2026
Editorial illustration

For calcium carbide production, the carbon material must be discussed together with the lime burden and furnace practice. The useful comparison is not simply one supplier name against another; it is one agreed specification and delivered size distribution against the plant’s operating requirement.

Confirm the burden basis

Share the furnace route, lime quality, burden ratio and target carbide grade. Review fixed carbon, ash, sulfur, phosphorus, moisture and volatile matter against that context.

Control size and fines

Select a particle-size range and define fines and oversize limits when they affect charging or bed permeability. Confirm packing, storage and the sampling point used for acceptance.

Build the order around evidence

Request an agreed COA format and state any third-party inspection or retained-sample requirement. Catalog figures remain reference values until the order records the final specification and quality-control procedure.
